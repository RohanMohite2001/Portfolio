I am a passionate Unity Developer with nearly 2 years of hands-on experience in designing and building interactive applications and games. My expertise lies in Unity3D, where I specialize in gameplay programming, performance optimization, and creating engaging user experiences.

I enjoy solving complex programming challenges, working on game mechanics, and continuously learning new technologies. With a strong problem-solving mindset and a detail-oriented approach, I strive to write high-quality, maintainable code and contribute to building innovative, polished products.
